Reading List

Books that have shaped my coaching philosophy and that I frequently recommend to clients.

Charles Duhigg

The Power of Habit

Delves into how habits emerge within individual lives and is based on hundreds of interviews, and thousands more papers and studies.

Dr. Russ Harris

The Confidence Gap

An insightful read for those who want to work on increasing confidence. The ideas are based on Acceptance and Commitment Training (ACT).

Herminia Ibarra

Working Identity

A must read for anyone contemplating changing careers to attain greater congruence between what they do and who they are.
